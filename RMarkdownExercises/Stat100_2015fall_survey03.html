Stat 100 Survey 3, Fall 2015

1045 Responses, 15 Variables

Column Description

  1. gender: What is your gender?
  2. genderID: What gender do you identify with at this current moment?
  3. ethnicity: Which of the following groups best describes your racial/ethnic background. (Christian, Jewish, Muslim, Hindu, Buddhist, Religious but not one of the above, Agnostic, Atheist)
  4. marijuana: Should Marijuana be legalized nationwide?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means you Strongly favor the nationwide legalization of marijuana ... "10" means you Strongly oppose the nationwide legalization of marijuana
  5. politics: Which way do you lean politically?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means Extremely liberal .... "10" means Extremely conservative.
  6. poverty: In your opinion, which is generally to blame if a person is poor in this country? Circumstances beyond his or her control or lack of effort on his or her part?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate your opinion:
    "0" means Circumstances beyond the person's control ........ "10" means lack of effort.
  7. temperament: Do you consider yourself an Introvert or Extrovert or Ambivert (combo of both)?
  8. president: If the presidential election was tomorrow, who would you vote for out of these candidates? (Hilary Clinton, Bernie Sanders, Joe Biden, Martin O'Malley, Lincoln Chaffee, Donald Trump, Jeb Bush, Ted Cruz, Marco Rubio, Chris Christie, Ben Carson, Carly Fiorina, Other)
  9. cavities: How many cavities have you had total in your life?
  10. gayMarriage: On a scale of 0-10 do you favor or oppose allowing gays and lesbians to marry legally? (0 means strongly favor gay marriage and 10 means strongly oppose gay marriage.)
  11. triedGaySex: Have you, yourself, ever happened to try a same sex experience?
  12. gunControl: How strict should gun control laws be?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means the purchase of firearms should be very lightly regulated ... "10" means that the purchase of firearms should be heavily regulated.
  13. parentsDivorced: Have any of your parents/guardians ever been divorced?
  14. happiness: How happy are you on a scale from 0 to 10? ("0" = the least happy ...........and 10 = the happiest)
  15. collegeEnjoyment: On a scale of 0-10, how much do you enjoy college? (0 = you hate it ...........and 10 = the best experience of your life)
