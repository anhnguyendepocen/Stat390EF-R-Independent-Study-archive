Galton's Height Data

898 cases, 6 Columns

Column Description

  1. Family: The family that the child belongs to, labeled by the numbers from 1 to 204 and 136A
  2. Father: The father's height, in inches
  3. Mother: The mother's height, in inches
  4. Gender: The gender of the child, male (M) or female (F)
  5. Height: The height of the child, in inches
  6. Kids: The number of kids in the family of the child

Source: Galton's Height Data from http://www.randomservices.org
