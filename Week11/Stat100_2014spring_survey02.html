Stat 100 Survey 2, Spring 2014

858 Responses, 26 Variables

Column Description

  1. gender: What is your gender?
  2. greek: Are you a member of a fraternity of sorority?
  3. homeTown: Are you from a small town, a medium sized city (like Champaign Urbana), a big city suburb (like Chicago suburb) or from a big city (like chicago or STL, but not the suburbs)?
  4. ethnicity: Which of the following groups best describes your racial/ethnic background. If you're a mixture and have a primary identification, choose that one. If not, choose mixed.(Categories borrowed from US Census.)
  5. religion: Which of the following groups best describes your religious identification?
  6. religious: On a scale of 0-10, how religious are you? (0 means not at all religious and 10 means extremely religious.)
  7. ACT: What score did you get on the ACT? (If you took the SAT then translate your score into an ACT, see SAT-to- ACT conversion.)
  8. GPA: What is your GPA in college so far? (If you're a freshman give your high school GPA.)
  9. partyHr: About how many hours do you party per week on the average?
  10. drinks: About how many alcoholic drinks do you consume per week on the average?
  11. sexPartners: How many people have you slept with in your life?
  12. relationships: How many people have you been in a serious relationship with?
  13. firstKissAge: How old were you the first time you kissed someone in a romantic way?
  14. favLifePeriod: What has been the favorite period of your life so far? (0-Kindergarten, 1st-5th grades, middle school, high school, college)
  15. hoursParents: About how many times a week do you talk on the phone to either of your parents?
  16. tweets: About how many tweets do you send out a week?
  17. texts: How many texts do you send per day on average?
  18. gayMarriage: On a scale of 0-10 do you favor or oppose allowing gays and lesbians to marry legally? (0 means strongly favor gay marriage and 10 means strong oppose gay marriage.)
  19. triedGaySex: Have you, yourself, ever happened to try a same sex experience?
  20. abortion: On a scale of 0-10, what comes closest to your views about abortion? (0 means a woman should be able to get an abortion if she decides she wants one no matter what the reason and 10 means a woman should never be able to get an abortion no matter what.)
  21. politics: Which way do you lean politically?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means Extremely liberal and.........."10" means Extremely conservative)
  22. poverty: In your opinion, which is generally to blame if a person is poor in this country? Circumstances beyond his or her control or lack of effort on his or her part?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate your opinion:
    "0" means Circumstances beyond the person's control ........and "10" means lack of effort
  23. goodOrWell: When you graduate are you more interested in "doing good" (helping others) or "doing well" (gaining material resoures)?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means "doing good" ........and"10" means "doing well"
  24. parentRelationship: On a scale of 0-10, how well do you get along with your parents? (0 means "not at all" and 10 means "extremely well".)
  25. workHr: About how many hours a week do you work at a paying job?
  26. tuition: About what percent of your college costs are your parents paying for?
