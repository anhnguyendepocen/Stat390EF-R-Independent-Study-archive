Stat 100 and Stat 200 Survey 4, Fall 2017 (Flanagan & Fireman)

1518 responses, 40 columns

Data Columns

  1. gender: When you were born, what was your biological sex?

  2. genderID: What gender do you identify with at this current moment? (Male/Female/Other)

  3. ethnicity: Which of the following groups best describes your racial/ethnic background?
    White
    Black or African American
    Hispanic/Latino
    East Asian (including China, Korea, Japan, Thailand, Vietnam,Laos, Indonesia etc.)
    South Asian (including India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, etc.)
    Mixed/Other

  4. citizen Are you a US citizen?

  5. English: Is English your native language?

  6. Exam1: What score did you get on Exam 1?

  7. Exam2: What score did you get on Exam 2?

  8. attraction: What percent of all U of I students that you see would you estimate that you are physically attracted to?

  9. Muslim:
    Version 1: Would you support extreme vetting of Muslim immigrants even if it kept most Muslims out?
    Version 2: Would you support a ban on Muslim immigrants with exceptions for those who meet special standards?

  10. religious: How religious are you — on a scale from 0 (not religious) to 10 (very religious)?

  11. conservative_self: How socially conservative are you — on a scale from 0 (very liberal) to 10 (very conservative)?

  12. conservative_parents: How socially conservative are your parents — on a scale from 0 (very liberal) to 10 (very conservative)?

  13. sexualOrientation: What's your sexual orientation? (straight, gay/lesbian, bi, asexual, other)

  14. familyKnowsSexualOrientation: Does your family know your sexual orientation? (No, Yes, Some do)

  15. age_sexualOrientation: How old were you when you first became aware of your sexual orientation?

  16. changeSexualOrientation: Do you think your sexual orientation is likely to change in the future?

  17. sameSexExperience: Have you ever had a same-sex experience?

  18. dateTransgender: Would you date someone who is transgender?

  19. MFS_self: Male/female spectrum
    Version 1: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0=completely masculine and 10=completely feminine), how do you feel on the inside?
    Version 2: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0=completely feminine and 10=completely masculine), how do you feel on the inside?
    Note: Scales in version 2 have been reserved in the data to be consistent with version 1.

  20. MFS_present: Male/female spectrum
    Version 1: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0=completely masculine and 10=completely feminine), how do you present yourself to others?
    Version 2: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0=completely feminine and 10=completely masculine), how do you present yourself to others?
    Note: Scales in version 2 have been reserved in the data to be consistent with version 1.

  21. MFS_attraction: Male/female spectrum
    Version 1: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0=completely masculine and 10=completely feminine), who are you sexually attracted to?
    Version 2: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0=completely feminine and 10=completely masculine), who are you sexually attracted to?
    Note: Scales in version 2 have been reserved in the data to be consistent with version 1.

  22. gayFeelings: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0 is totally accept and 10 is totally oppose) what are your feelings about homosexuality between men?
    In version 2: the order of male and female homosexuality questions is switched.

  23. lesbianFeelings: On a scale from 0 to 10 (0 is totally accept and 10 is totally oppose) what are your feelings about homosexuality between women?
    In version 2: the order of male and female homosexuality questions is switched.

  24. sexPartners: How many people have you slept with? (50=50 or more)

  25. relationship: How many people have you been in serious relationships with? (10=10 or more)

  26. sexInSeriousRelationship: How important is sex in a serious relationship?
    On a scale of 0 to 10... 0=not important at all ...... 10-it's all-important.

  27. seriousSex: How important is being serious in a sexual relationship?
    On a scale of 0 to 10... 0=not important at all ...... 10-it's all-important.

  28. sexBeforeMarriage: Would you have sex before marriage? (No, Unsure, Yes - I would, Yes - I already have)

  29. children: How many children would you ideally like to have?

  30. abortion: Version 1: Which comes closest to your view on whether or not a woman should have the right to end her own pregnancy: she should always have the right; she should have the right most of the time; she should only have the right in cases of rape, incest and to save her own life; or she should never have the right, no matter what?
    Version 2: Which comes closest to your view on abortion: abortion should always be legal; should be legal most of the time; should be made illegal except in cases of rape, incest and to save the mother's life; or abortion should be made illegal without any exceptions?
    (Choices: always legal / legal most of the time / neutral / Illegal except in cases of rape, incests and to save the life of the mother / Illegal without exception)

  31. bed: Do you make your bed most days?

  32. birthMonth: What month were you born in?

  33. TProll: There are 2 ways to place a roll of toilet paper on a horizontal holder. Which method do you believe is best? (under/over/no preference)

  34. TPconfig: There are 2 basic ways to configure the toilet paper: WAD or FOLD. Which way do you believe is best?

  35. foodPreference: Would you rather eat at Burger King or McDonald's?

  36. burgers: Would you rather spend a night with the Burger King and "Have it your way".....OR.....spend a night with Ronald McDonald and realize you were "Lovin' it".

  37. innocentVsGuilty: Would you rather have one innocent person executed for a crime they didn't commit (obviously), or have ten guilty people (rapists, murders, child molesters) get off scot-free?

  38. sexVsLove: Would you rather to never have sex or never find true love?

  39. attendance: Would you rather that no one show up to your Wedding.....OR.....that no one show up to your Funeral

  40. version: Survey version 1 or 2 (same questions but phrased slightly differently).
