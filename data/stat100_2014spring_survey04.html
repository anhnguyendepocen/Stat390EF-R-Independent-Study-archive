Stat 100 Survey 4, Spring 2014

922 responses, 17 variables

Column Description

  1. version: Version 0 or 1
  2. gender: What is your gender?
  3. ethnicity: Which of the following groups best describes your racial/ethnic background?
    White
    Black or African American
    Hispanic/Latino
    Asian (including Far East, Southeast and the Indian subcontinent, India, Pakistan, etc.)
    Other
  4. expectedIncome: Twenty years down the road, how much money do you expect to make per year?
  5. healthCare
    Version 0: What are your feelings toward the Affordable Care Act?
    Version 1: What are your feelings toward Obamacare?
  6. flightSearch
    Version 0: Do you think that the American government should continue spending millions of dollars on searching for the missing Flight 370?
    Version 1: Do you think that the American government should continue spending millions of dollars on searching for the 227 missing passengers on Flight 370?
  7. TProll: There are 2 ways to place a roll of toilet paper on a horizontal holder. Which method do you believe is best? (under/over/no preference)
  8. TPconfig: There are 2 basic ways to configure the toilet paper: WAD or FOLD. Which way do you believe is best?
  9. foodPreference: Would you rather eat at Burger King or McDonald's?
  10. burgers: Would you rather wake up naked and sore and without any memory of the night before, next to the Burger King telling you "you had it your way" or wake up next to Ronald McDonald telling you that "you were loving it"?
  11. innocentVsGuilty: Would you rather have one innocent person executed for a crime they didn't commit (obviously), or have ten guilty people (rapists, murders, child molesters) get off scot-free?
  12. animalVsLanguage: Would you rather be able to talk with all animals or be able to speak all foreign languages
  13. vision: Would you rather see everything blurry or see everything in black and white?
  14. sexVsLove: Would you rather to never have sex or never find true love?
  15. abortion: Which comes closest to your view on abortion: abortion should always be legal; should be legal most of the time; should be made illegal except in cases of rape, incest and to save the mother's life; or abortion should be made illegal without any exceptions?
    (Always Legal, Usually Legal, Neutral, Rarely Legal, Always Legal)
  16. gayMarriage: What is your opinion on allowing gay and lesbian couples to marry legally? (Strongly Favor, Favor, Neutral, Oppose, Strongly Oppose)
  17. deathPenalty: Are you in favor of the death penalty for a person convicted of murder? (Strongly Oppose, Oppose, Neutral, Favor, Strongly Favor)
