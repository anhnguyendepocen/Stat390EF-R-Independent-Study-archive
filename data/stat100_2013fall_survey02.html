Stat 100 Survey 2, Fall 2013

893 responses, 26 questions

Column Description

  1. gender: What is your gender?
  2. schoolYear: What year are you in college?
  3. greek: Are you a member of a fraternity of sorority?
  4. homeTown: Are you from a small town, a medium sized city (like Champaign Urbana), a big city suburb (like Chicago suburb) or from a big city (like chicago or STL, but not the suburbs)?
  5. ethnicity: Which of the following groups best describes your racial/ethnic background: White, Black or African American, Hispanic/Latino, Asian, Mixed or Other
  6. religion: Which of the following groups best describes your religious identification?
    Christian, Jewish, Muslim, Hindu, Buddhist, Religious but not one of the above, Agnostic, Atheist
  7. religious: How religious are you?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means not at all religious and "10" means extremely regious.
  8. ACT: What score did you get on the ACT?
  9. GPA: What is your GPA in college so far? (If freshman, give high school GPA.)
  10. partyHr: On average, how many hours per week do you party?
  11. drinks: On average, how many alcoholic drinks do you consume per week?
  12. sexPartners: How many sex partners have you had in your life? (50 = 50 or more)
  13. relationships: How many people have you been in a serious relationship with?
  14. firstKissAge: How old were you the first time you kissed someone in a romantic way?
  15. hoursCallParents: About how many times a week do you talk on the phone to either of your parents?
  16. socialMedia: About how many hours a day total would you estimate you spend on social media (facebook, twitter, instagram, etc.)?
  17. Syria: Congress is about to vote on whether to authorize missile strikes against the Syrian government. On a scale of 0-10 are you for or against missile strikes? 0 means definitely against missile strikes and 10 means definitely for missile strikes.
  18. gayMarriage: On a scale of 0-10 do you favor or oppose allowing gays and lesbians to marry legally? (0 means strongly favor gay marriage and 10 means strong oppose gay marriage.)
  19. triedGaySex: Have you, yourself, ever happened to try a same sex experience?
  20. abortion: On a scale of 0-10, what comes closest to your views about abortion? (0 means a woman should be able to get an abortion if she decides she wants one no matter what the reason and 10 means a woman should never be able to get an abortion no matter what.)
  21. politics: Which way do you lean politically?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means Extremely liberal and.........."10" means Extremely conservative)
  22. poverty: In your opinion, which is generally to blame if a person is poor in this country? Circumstances beyond his or her control or lack of effort on his or her part?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate your opinion:
    "0" means Circumstances beyond the person's control ........and "10" means lack of effort
  23. goodOrWell: When you graduate are you more interested in "doing good" (helping others) or "doing well" (gaining material resoures)?
    On a scale of 0-10 rate where you fall:
    "0" means "doing good" ........and"10" means "doing well"
  24. parentRelationship: On a scale of 0-10, how well do you get along with your parents? (0 means "not at all" and 10 means "extremely well".)
  25. workHr: About how many hours a week do you work at a paying job?
  26. tuition: About what percent of your college costs are your parents paying for?
