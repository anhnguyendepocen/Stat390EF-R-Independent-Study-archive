Stat 100 Survey 1 Fall 2010

963 responses, 14 variables

Column Description

  1. gender: What is your gender?
  2. Age: What is your age, in years?
  3. height: What is your height? (in inches)
  4. weight: What is your weight, in pounds?
  5. Pets: How many dogs plus cats have you and your family owned in your lifetime?
  6. Speed: What is the fastest you have ever driven a car, in miles per hour?
  7. Cash: Approximately how much cash do you have with you now, in dollars? Limit was $1000
  8. Sleep: On average, how many hours of sleep do you get each night?
  9. Shoes: How many pairs of shoes do you own?
  10. Charity: Suppose you won a million dollars and were given the choice to either spend it on yourself or give it away to save the lives of children who would otherwise die of starvation. The more money you gave, the more children would be saved. What percent of the money would you give away?
  11. Cheat: How many times have you ever cheated on an exam in school?
  12. Greek: Are you a member of a fraternity of sorority?
  13. Handed: Are you left-handed, right-handed, or ambidextrous?
  14. Soulmate: Do you believe there is only one person in the world who's your true love, or do you think there are many who could be? How many people in the world do you think you could truly love? (none, one, a few, dozens, hundreds, thousands, millions ?)
